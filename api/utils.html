s2fft.utils

s2fft.utils.flm_1d_to_2d(flm_1d: numpy.ndarray, L: int) → numpy.ndarray

Converts from 1d indexed flms to 2d indexed

Conventions for e.g. \(L = 3\)

\[\begin{split}2D = \begin{bmatrix} flm_{(2,-2)} & flm_{(2,-1)} & flm_{(2,0)} & flm_{(2,1)} & flm_{(2,2)} \\ 0 & flm_{(1,-1)} & flm_{(1,0)} & flm_{(1,1)} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & flm_{(0,0)} & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[1D = [flm_{0,0}, flm_{1,-1}, flm_{1,0}, flm_{1,1}, \dots]\]
Returns

2D indexed flms

s2fft.utils.flm_2d_to_1d(flm_2d: numpy.ndarray, L: int) → numpy.ndarray

Converts from 2d indexed flms to 1d indexed

Conventions for e.g. \(L = 3\)

\[\begin{split}2D = \begin{bmatrix} flm_{(2,-2)} & flm_{(2,-1)} & flm_{(2,0)} & flm_{(2,1)} & flm_{(2,2)} \\ 0 & flm_{(1,-1)} & flm_{(1,0)} & flm_{(1,1)} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & flm_{(0,0)} & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[1D = [flm_{0,0}, flm_{1,-1}, flm_{1,0}, flm_{1,1}, \dots]\]
Returns

1D indexed flms
